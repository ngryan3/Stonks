Goal of the Project

The goal of this project is to see how crypto-currency can be used to diversify investor portfolios. We will look at ways we can optimize portfolios using crypto and how they compare to traditional portfolios. Also analyze the differences between cryptocurrency and stocks (i.e. differences in how their prices move and volatility.)

Click on the Get Started button to find the optimal portfolio for the stonks you selected!
